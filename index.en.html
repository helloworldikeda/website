Scratch Level Details

Level 1

Scratch Intro

Sprites, Scripts, Loops

A course for beginners. Learn how to add code, costumes, and sounds to sprites as you create animations, games, stories, and music.

More Scratch

Broadcasts, Conditionals, Variables

Build on the basics. Use broadcasting, conditionals like "if-then", and variables to create apps, games, and simulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the trial lesson really free?

Yes — trial lessons are completely free. There are no enrollment or material fees for the trial.

Is it okay if my child has never studied English?

Absolutely. We offer level-based classes and beginner-friendly options.

Can I reschedule a lesson?

Yes — if you contact us by the day before, you can reschedule up to twice per month.

What age can children start programming?

Programming lessons are available from 1st grade (elementary school). If they can use a mouse, they can start.

What if we don't have a computer?

You can use our classroom PCs for a small rental fee. For online lessons, please prepare a device.

Is coding still relevant for kids in the AI era?

Yes, more than ever. While AI is a powerful tool, it requires human creativity and logic to guide it. Learning to code builds the problem-solving skills needed to master technology and become the innovators of the future.
